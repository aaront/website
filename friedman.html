Elliote Friedman Team Abbreviation Generator

by Matthew Barlowe


Are you a sports journalist on twitter who needs to talk about NHL teams but is constrained by a tweet's character limits? Do you also want to completely confuse your readers as to what the actual teams you are talking about are? Well you're in luck! Just try out our patented Elliote Friedman NHL Team Abbreviation Generator™ or as well like to call it the ELFRDNLTABRGEN. Just rolls off the tongue doesn't it?

So how does it work you ask? It's easy peasey just select the team you want an abbreviation for and hit the button and you'll have your very own Elliote Friedman Abbreviation™. In no time at all you'll have all your followers in your mentions yelling at you just like the real Elliote Friedman. Any aspiring hockey writer can't live without it!
